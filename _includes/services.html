About

I am a Roboticist and Entrepreneur who co-founded Scaled Robotics to modernize construction. Construction a process which addresses one of the most fundamental of human needs, shelter, is currently plagued by waste and inefficiency. At Scaled Robotics, we are on a mission to modernize 21st century construction with the help of Robotics and A.I to make the industry less polluting and more efficient. You can find out more about my views on construction here I received my Ph.D in Computer Science from the University of Southern California under supervision of Prof. Stefan Schaal.. I also have masters degrees from University of Southern California, University of Pennsylvania and University of Maryland College Park. Over the last decade I have worked on ground, aerial and humanoid robotics. My research insterests lie in how to get intelligent systems to move and interact with their environment in order to build better models of their environment.
